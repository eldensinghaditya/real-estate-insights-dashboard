[Real Estate Insights Dashboard (Power BI)]  
This project analyzes housing prices and key property features to understand how different attributes influence market value. |
The dashboard provides interactive insights for real estate operations, pricing analysis, and listing coordination.

[Dataset]
Rows: 545 |
Columns: Price, Area, Bedrooms, Bathrooms, Stories, Main Road, Guestroom, Basement, Hot Water Heating, Air Conditioning, Parking, Preferred Area, Furnishing Status |
Source: Kaggle Housing Dataset

Key Insights:
1. Feature Impact on Price
Homes with air conditioning have significantly higher average prices.
Properties with basements are priced noticeably higher.
Homes with a guestroom command a clear price premium.
Preferred-area homes show strong price uplift.

2. Structural Factors
Price increases consistently with number of bedrooms and bathrooms.
Homes with more stories show higher average pricing.

3. Area vs Price
Strong positive relationship between property area and price.
Outliers represent luxury/large homes with additional features.

4. Furnishing Status
Furnished and semi-furnished homes tend to be priced higher than unfurnished ones.
